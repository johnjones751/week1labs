All Dogsa Go To Heaven

All Dogs Go to Heaven" (1989) is an animated film about Charlie, a street-smart German Shepherd who is killed by his former friend, Carface. After escaping Heaven, Charlie returns to Earth and befriends an orphaned girl, Anne-Marie, who can talk to animals. As Charlie seeks revenge on Carface, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and redemption. Ultimately, Charlie sacrifices his selfish desires to protect Anne-Marie, earning a second chance at life and proving that love and selflessness are the true rewards.
